Our Services

Hydraulic Steel Structures

Design, Fabrication, Installation and Commissioning of Hydraulic Steel Structures including, Gates, Stoplogs and Trash Racks for Hydro Power Projects, Dams, Barrages, Canals and small water distribution structures.

Tanks and Vessels

Design, Fabrication and Installation of Steel tanks for the storage of Fuel, LPG and Water etc. We also design and Fabricate the Pressure Vessels as per ASME Standards.


Hydro Power Projects

Design, Fabrication and Installation of Penstocks and allied equipment including, Flexible Joints, Bends, Reducers, Flanges etc.

Water and Sewage Pumping Stations

Design, Fabrication and Installation of Screens, Suction and Discharge Headers, dismantling Joints and other pipefittings and specials

Water and Sewage Treatment Plants

Design Fabrication and Installation of Following Equipment for Water and Sewage Treatment Plants.

  1. Water Control Gates
  2. Screens
  3. Sludge thickeners
  4. Scrappers and Scrapper Bridges
  5. Flocculators
  6. Mixers
  7. Water Filtration Nozzles
  8. Pipe Fittings and Specials
  9. Storage Tanks for Chemical House etc.
